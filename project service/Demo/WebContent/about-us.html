OUR MOTIVE

 

The service industry is pretty unorganized. Whether the customer needs a plumber or a carpenter, they need to go through several Google searches and referrals before they can find one. The laborers or hourly wagers too have to wait endlessly before they can get the paid opportunity. In some cases, the wager would sit for hours together and not find a single opportunity. This leads to wastage of resources and unavailability of services providers on time. while there are plenty of jobs, people seem to be unavailable and vice versa.The mordern job industry requires an organized structure and better opportunities. There is a growing need for different types of service providers. The Home Services application will help connect the service provider with the customer who needs it thus offering better opportunities, more options, and convenience to both parties involved.


An interesting business model is brought up by Home Services. The app hires expert professionals to provide services in areas like Home cleaning, Babysitting, Plumbing, AC repairing and maintaining, Carpentry, Driver on Demand, Laundry, Pest Control, Salon at Home, Car Spa, electrical repair, PC & Laptop Repair, and House Painting services, as well as complete home refresh packages. You just say what you want and it will be done. With over 1000 service professionals registered in the app, Home Services allows users to find the right people for all their needs and get a great service done, allowing you to hire a trusted professional when you want at the price you can afford. 

  • User selects the service they need.
  • Checks list of available professionals.
  • See cost estimates and detailed business information.
  • Compare prices, read reviews and book service professionals.

 

Once the Service gets completed, the customer can pay the service provider on the spot via any payment method suitable to them(cash, debit / credit card, upi etc.) . Also the customer need to mark the booked service as completed in the app.
